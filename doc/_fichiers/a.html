        src/clojure_noob/core.clj is where we'll be doing our Clojure coding for awhile.

        resources is a place for you to store assets like images; we won't be using it for awhile.

        The word awhile is not a noun but an adverb meaning "for a short time," and substituting it in the above highlighted text yields a redundant preposition: "for for a short time." The noun phrase "a while"--two words--is the proper phrase to use in those contexts, hence "for a while." See the Oxford English Dictionary for details.
